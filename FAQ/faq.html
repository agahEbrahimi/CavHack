  • Basics

  • What is CavHack?

    CavHack is a hackathon designed and run by students from W.T. Woodson High Shool for students to learn how to code and work with others to make new technologies.

  • When is CavHack?

    The hackathon will take place on a weekend at the beginning of December. Exact dates to come soon.

  • Will there be prizes?

    Yes, there will multiple prize categories for participants to compete in.

  • Is it going to be fun?

    Of course it's going to be fun! What kind of a question is that?

  • Registration

  • Who can register?

    Secondary students in FCPS are eligible to attend.

  • Is there any cost to register?

    No, it's completely free!

  • Is there a deadline for registration?

    There is no deadline for registration, but you should register soon before all our openings are filled!

  • Attendance

  • What do you mean by hacking?

    In this situation, our "hacking" is developing a project over the course of the event. It is NOT the illegal hacking of gaining unauthorized access to information.

  • What if I don't know how to code?

    No problem! In fact, this Hackathon is meant for you! Our goal is to take students with little coding experience and teach them how to code and make applications.

  • Is there a code of conduct?

    Yes, there is a code of conduct which you can read here.

  • Do I need a team?

    You can form your own team priot to the event, but it's no problem if you come without a team! For those wishing to work in teams, we will help you develop one during the event.

  • Needs

  • Will there be food?

    Yes, there will be LOTS of food!

  • What should I bring?

    You will need to bring your laptop, phone, chargers, a sleeping bag/pillow, toiletries, and extra clothing.

  • What if I have dietary concerns?

    Food will be provided for everyone. If you have special concerns please contact us here.
