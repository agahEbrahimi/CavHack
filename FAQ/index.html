  • Basics

  • What is CavHack?

    CavHack is a Hackathon designed and run by Students from W.T.Woodson High Shool, where students learn how to code and work with others to make new technologies.

  • When is CavHack?

    The start of the Hackathon is Saturday, December 2 and ends on Sunday, December 3.

  • Will there be prizes?

    Yes, there will be prizes for winners in different divisions.

  • Is it going to be fun?

    Of course it's going to be fun. What kind of a question is that?

  • Registration

  • Who can register?

    All students in middle school and high school in Virginia can attend

  • Is there any cost to registration?

    No, it's completely free

  • Is there a deadline for registration?

    There is no deadline for registration but there's a possibility of the slots being filled

  • Attendance

  • What do you mean by hacking?

    Hacking is making something through the course of the event and not the illegal hacking which is gaining unauthorized access to information.

  • What if I don't know how to code?

    It's alright, this Hackathon is meant for you, you can learn how to code and make applications with us

  • Is there a code of conduct?

    Yes, there is a code of conduct which you can read Here

  • Do I need a team?

    You can make your own team before the event but don't worry if you don't have a team. We will help you get one in the event.

  • Needs

  • Will there be food?

    Yes, there will be lots of food

  • What should I bring?

    You'll need to bring your laptop, phone, chargers, a sleeping bag/pillow, toiletries, and extra clothing.

  • What if I have dietary concerns?

    Food will be provided for everyone. If you have special concerns then Contact Us
